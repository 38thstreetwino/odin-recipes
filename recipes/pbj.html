Peanut Butter & Jelly

The classic sandwhich we all ate as a kid, just this time with the crust

Ingredients
  • Whole wheat bread slices
  • Crunchy peanut butter
  • Strawberry preserves
Steps
  1. Prepare two slices of whole wheat bread
  2. Coat one slice of bread with a thin layer of the peanut butter
  3. Coat the other slice with a thin layer of preserves
  4. Place the first slice on top of the second so that the peanut butter and jelly touch
  5. Enjoy the nostalgia
