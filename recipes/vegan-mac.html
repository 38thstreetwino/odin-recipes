Vegan Macaroni

Chilli style macaroni packed with fresh veggies, vegan sausage, and flavor!

Ingredients
  • Whole wheat macaroni
  • Vegan sausage ground
  • Spinach
  • Red bell pepper
  • Paprika
  • Chilli powder
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red onion
Steps
  1. Prepare the macaroni according to package instructions; set aside
  2. Dice the bell pepper, red onion, and garlic
  3. Add olive oil and diced garlic to a pan on medium heat
  4. After garlic becomes aromatic, add diced bell pepper
  5. Cook bell pepper for about 3 minutes, then add the sausage ground, paprika, and chili powder
  6. Break the sausage up and cook until lightly browned
  7. Add macaroni and stir together
  8. Add spinach; stir in as it wilts and remove from heat when dark green and wilted down
