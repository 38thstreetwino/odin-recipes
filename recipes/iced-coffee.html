Iced Coffee

A simple way to shake up your morning cup of joe

Ingredients
  • Freshly ground coffee beans
  • Whole milk
  • Creamer; flavored and/or non-dairy if you prefer
  • Ice
Steps
  1. Prepare your coffe how you prefer; drip, French press, pour-over, etc
  2. In a short mixing glass, froth the milk and creamer together until foamy
  3. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, add the brewed coffee, and shake until cold
  4. Fill a tall glass with ice and pour the chilled coffee in
  5. Float the frothed milk & cream on top
